How many hours per week do I endure?

The interactive chart below displays the amount of time I have spent running, cycling, and pursuing other endurance sports such as swimming and hiking. For example, in June 2020 I hit nearly 20 hours per week of activity, and in the first week of December 2019 I barely ran more than two hours per week as I was preparing for an intense UChicago finals season.

Data is automatically fetched from Strava using the Strava API and GitHub Action. Visit the GitHub repository to see how I accomplish this. Click and drag on the canvas area behind the plot to focus on a smaller range of time. Double click the canvas to reset.
